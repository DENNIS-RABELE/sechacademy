SECH Academy: Bridging the Gap in Education and Sport Development

About Us

Sech Academy is a state-of-the-art center framed to offer ground-breaking teaching and learning in delivering and supporting both secondary education and sport development.

Vision

Leader in advanced educational methodologies and master in sport development.

Mission Statement

Relegating barriers towards learning and diversifying career paths.

Objectives

  • Achieve academic excellence and promote sport development for job creation.
  • Empower children and youth to develop a career through their talents.
  • Promote and support mental health.
  • Principal Values: Professionalism, Integrity, Transparency, and Accountability.
  • Inclusion and collaboration, creativity and innovation, high work ethic.

The SECH Way

Learner-centered lessons, cognitive series games, interactive tutorials, situational method in teaching, SECH e-tutor, positive reinforcement, and parental engagement.

Why Choose Us?

  • SECH Academy promotes good mental health in response to high orphanages, escalating divorce rates, domestic violence, etc.
  • We offer full residential accommodation to supervise learning habits and schedules beyond the classroom.
  • Sport development: We view sport beyond recreation. Football and other sports are promoted to harness natural athletic abilities for economic productivity and self-reliance.

Admissions

Admissions are open in Grade 8. We believe in setting our own foundation for the holistic development of our learners.

Curriculum

We offer the curriculum developed by the Ministry of Education and Training in Lesotho, with subjects that include French, English, Travel and Tourism, Life skills, Maths, Physical science, Sesotho, Agriculture, Development Studies.

Grading System

  • Pass with Distinction: 80% and above on a minimum of six subjects plus a pass in English language.
  • First Class: 70-79% aggregate score on a minimum of five compulsory subjects plus English.
  • Second Class: 60-69% aggregate score, including a minimum pass of 4 subjects (English included).
  • Ordinary Pass: 50-59% aggregate score, including a minimum of 4 subjects plus a pass in English language.
  • Promotion: 45-49% aggregate score, including a pass in a minimum of two compulsory subjects plus one elective subject.
  • Unsuccessful and Must Repeat: 44% and below.
